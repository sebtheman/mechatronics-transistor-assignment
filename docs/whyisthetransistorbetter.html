Why the transistor is better

.

The transistor is a lot better than valves in quite a few ways

  1. The transistor could be made very small, meaning that computers and other devicves wouldn't take up so much space

  2. It uses a lot less electricity than the valves, which is better for the environment and costs less. It also allows for battery-powered devices.

  3. The transistor is a lot more efficient, meaning that it gives off less heat and doesn't need to be cooled down as much as valves, which cuts down on costs.
